The Bocas del Toro region of Panama is changing. Some changes are subtle and slow, like the ebbing of social structures that divide Panamanians. Other changes — environmental, social and economic — can overtake entire islands and transform ways of living with the urgency of violent waves. These changes are fluid, permeable, constant. They are the undercurrents of Bocas del Toro.
