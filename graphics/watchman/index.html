The shrinking Zapatillas

The Zapatillas Keys, located in the Bastimentos Island National Marine Park, are the two islands where Chenco is conserving sea turtles, but the islands themselves are shrinking. Today, the larger island is 34 hectares, while the smaller is 14 hectares. Scroll through the timeline and watch the shoreline change.
